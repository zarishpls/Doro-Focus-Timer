Why Use the Pomodoro Technique?

  • Improved Focus

    Short, focused work sessions help maintain concentration and reduce distractions.

  • Reduced Burnout

    Regular breaks prevent mental fatigue and help maintain productivity over time.

  • Better Time Management

    Breaking work into intervals helps you estimate task durations more accurately.

  • Increased Accountability

    Tracking completed Pomodoros provides a sense of accomplishment and progress.
